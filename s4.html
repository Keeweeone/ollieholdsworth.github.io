The brief

Sam wanted a film that felt like the car — composed, quick, and a little understated. Not a highlight reel, but a proper short film with a beginning, middle, and end.

Shot in one evening across South Auckland. Rolling road footage, in-car perspective, and tight detail work stitched together with a grade that lets the B9's lines do the talking.
